Pokemon Tabletop Move Index

This application is designed to be used while playing a Tabletop game - The application allows us to avoid going through libraries of data and instead provides this information instantly to the user, this makes playing a live tabletop game smoother and more enjoyable without having to micromanage information of your character, or in this case Pokemon

Inspiration and Audience

A tabletop surfaced fueled by fans inspired from the popular cartoon Pokemon which used fairly complicated rules as well as a bunch of individuals looking for ways to enhance the experience. Something I found that needed improvement was the pacing in the game. Fighting took hours at a time to do and it was mostly due to the fact that there were SO MANY moves that you could use on your opponents. This avenue inspired me to make this application which just serves as a index of all the moves that have been published by this game.

Project structure

In order to make a proper GUI for java I wanted to make something that was made off of the Model, View, Controller infrastructure. The only reason why is because its easy to organize your programs if all of your functions are partitioned and organized; especially if your going to be doing this with a group. In particular I was assisted with publication by Brian Tate
